Mirabé

For over a decade, at the end of Avenida Tibidabo a splendid balcony over Barcelona offers a spectacular setting to enjoy the best views of the city. Mirabé is a very special place ideal for watching the sunset, enjoying a a nice dinner or having a good gin and tonic.

Elegant and warm, the top floor of the space has a large window with a long bar that offers a stunning view of the city. The lower floor, which along with the garden can be hired for private celebrations works as a dance floor. On the other hand, the garden boasts a nice landscape –there is even a pond– and has a terrace that lit up, in the evening, is a magical setting.

Location: Tibidabo

Dining room capaciy: 300 pax

Price Level: Medium-high

Style: Mediterranean cuisine

Special diets: Available

Handicapped facilities: Yes
