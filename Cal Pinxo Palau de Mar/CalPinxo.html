Cal Pinxo Palau de Mar

In a place like the huge Palau de Mar in Port Vell, so packed with restaurants, Cal Pinxo stands out from the rest thanks to the excellent traditional Catalan cuisine that focuses on fish dishes.

The restaurant is elegant and, regardless of the time, we will find ourselves in an intimate cosy place where we will feel really at ease. Besides, the restaurant offers a wonderful terrace where every single meal becomes a nice time with the Mediterranean sea and Montjuic as backdrop. There is also a private room that holds up to 40 people.

Cal Pinxo's menu is fresh and varied, and invites us to enjoy the real paellas, fideuas, fresh fish and seafood, always with the best quality and simply elaborated with dedication.

Location: Port Vell

Dining room capacity: Dinning rooms for 40 and 60 pax

Price level: Medium-high

Style: Mediterranean cuisine

Special diets: Available

Handicapped facilities: Yes
